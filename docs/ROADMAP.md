# Roadmap

## Next Engineering Milestones

1. Backend API with listings, users, roommate quiz profiles, saved searches, and moderation states.
2. LLM extraction endpoint for pasted posts and source imports.
3. Embedding store for AI Finder, duplicate detection, and roommate compatibility.
4. Geocoding integration with distance to any UW building.
5. Source adapters with compliance checks and rate limiting.
6. Admin review queue for duplicates, scams, expired posts, and low-confidence extraction.
7. Saved filters, alerts, and seasonal launch pages for study-abroad and semester turnover windows.

## Product Ideas

- "Move-in math" panel that explains rent, signing bonuses, utilities, and effective monthly cost.
- Scam-risk indicators for suspicious pricing, missing address, duplicated images, and off-platform payment requests.
- Compare drawer for shortlisting apartments, roommates, and furniture bundles.
- Campus building presets for students who want to optimize around a specific lecture hall or lab.
